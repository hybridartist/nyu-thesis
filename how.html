How

Research and design methods

In this thesis I research, design, and implement a system of wayfinding in mixed reality environments. The project leverages cues from nature to provide a more intuitive wayfinding user experience across physical and digital objects that coexist and interact.

Research

Further user research including cognitive mapping and contextual inquiry will be used to understand user information needs in context. Rapid prototyping and user testing will achieve a more human-centered design of mixed reality experiences.

Technology

While there are many options for development, the focus of my thesis is on researching and testing a wayfinding system in context. To this end I plan to use beam-splitting glass to project images from an iPad in order to quickly develop, test, and iterate upon designs.
