How

Research and design methods

In this thesis I propose to research and develop frameworks for user advocacy in the design of ubiquitous computing environments. By conducting interviews with designers, strategists, and other stakeholders I aim to uncover insights most relevant for the human-centered design of these systems and visualize them for accessible use by practitioners in the field.
