The Value of UX Research

Software project failures cost the US economy an estimated $25 to $75 billion annually, and many of the reasons for failure are preventable. UX research conducted up front can help avoid some of these failures such as badly defined system requirements, poor communication among customers, users and developers, and stakeholder politics.
